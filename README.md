# LEGO Siege Weapons 🧱⚔️

Build powerful, functional siege weapons entirely from LEGO Technic and standard bricks. Each design includes a complete parts list, step-by-step assembly instructions, tuning guides, and ammunition options.

These aren't display models — they actually fire projectiles across a room.

---

## The Arsenal

### [The Siege Breaker — Trebuchet](lego-trebuchet-build-guide.md)
A large-scale counterweight trebuchet with a composite double-beam arm, 1:4 pivot ratio, massive hinged counterweight (40–50 bricks), and a tuned sling for maximum range. Lobs projectiles 5–7 meters.

### [The Siege Striker — Ballista](lego-ballista-build-guide.md)
A dual-arm torsion ballista with reinforced torsion frames, 11-stud throwing arms, 8–12 rubber bands, a full-length walled bolt channel, and a bridge-style trigger. Fires bolts on a flat trajectory at 5–8 meters.

### [The Stone Storm — Catapult](lego-catapult-build-guide.md)
A torsion-powered onager-style catapult with a single throwing arm, padded crossbar stop, hinged scatter-shot bucket, and remote-fire trigger. Bombards targets at 5–8 meters with single stones or grapeshot.

## Quick Comparison

| | Trebuchet | Ballista | Catapult |
|---|---|---|---|
| Power source | Gravity (counterweight) | Torsion (rubber bands) | Torsion (rubber bands) |
| Trajectory | High arc | Flat / direct | High arc |
| Best for | Max range, heavy projectiles | Precision, single bolt | Area bombardment, scatter shot |
| Base size | ~32 x 20 studs | ~30 x 16 studs | ~30 x 20 studs |
| Range | 5–7 m | 5–8 m | 5–8 m |

## Safety

These builds launch projectiles with real force. Always:
- Aim away from people, pets, and breakable objects
- Use blunt-tipped ammo
- Wear eye protection during close-range testing
- Test outdoors or in a large open space
- Keep fingers clear of moving parts when cocked
